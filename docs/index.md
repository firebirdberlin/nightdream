Nightdream is a powerful **open-source desk clock** and versatile **screensaver (Daydream)**, perfect for both day and night use. The app features a **big digital clock** with auto-brightness, battery level, date, and notifications. The optimized night mode provides an extremely dark display (pure black on AMOLED) â€“ making it the ideal **night clock** for your bedside table.

## Customizable Design & Big Clock
Adapt colors, fonts, and analog watch faces to your liking. Easily resize the clock with a two-finger pinch-to-zoom â€“ perfect for a **full screen clock** experience. A classic **flip clock** style is also available for fans of retro designs.

## Clock Widget for Home Screen
Use the handy **clock widget** to keep track of time at a glance. Customize your home screen with a functional and aesthetic timepiece.

## Screensaver & Always on Display (AoD)
Use Nightdream as a **screensaver clock** (Daydream) on Android 4.2+. It works excellently as an **always on display clock**, which can automatically turn off at night and reactivate based on light. Transform your smartphone into an elegant **desk clock**.

## Alarms & Internet Radio
Set quick alarms with a simple swipe. Listen to your favorite internet radio stations and use them as your wake-up call. The app can manage Wi-Fi to ensure uninterrupted streaming for your **clock radio**.

## Smart Home Integration
Connect your **clock app** to your AVM smart home environment (Fritz!Box) to control lights or power outlets directly from your bedside.

## Battery Status & Notifications
Monitor your charging status and see the estimated time until fully charged directly on your **night clock**. Nightdream can also display notifications from your favorite apps (requires notification access).

## Weather Display
The app shows current weather and a 5-day forecast from reliable providers like OpenWeatherMap, Bright Sky, or Met.no.

## Extra Features of the Nightdream App
* Built-in flashlight for quick use at night.
* Optimized for a **digital clock wallpaper**-like look in full screen mode.
* Completely ad-free. Only basic functions are free.

## Open Source & Support
Nightdream is free, open-source software. The source code is public and open for contributions. Support development through a small donation or by unlocking premium features.
