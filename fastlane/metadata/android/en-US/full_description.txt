<b>Nightdream</b><br/>
An open-source desk clock and screensaver, perfect for day and night. It shows a digital clock with auto-brightness, battery level, date, and notifications. Night mode offers a dark display (pure black on AMOLED). Adjust font size with a two-finger zoom.
<br/><br/>
<b>Customizable Design</b><br/>
Adapt colors, fonts, and analog watch faces. Resize the clock with a two-finger zoom.
<br/><br/>
<b>Widgets</b><br/>
Add a clock widget to your home screen.
<br/><br/>
<b>Screensaver (Daydream)</b><br/>
Use as a screensaver on Android 4.2+. The screen can turn off at night and reactivate based on light or sound.
<br/><br/>
<b>Alarms &amp; Radio</b><br/>
Set quick alarms with a swipe. Listen to internet radio stations and use them for your alarm. The app can manage Wi-Fi for uninterrupted streaming.
<br/><br/>
<b>Smart Home Integration</b><br/>
Connect to your AVM smart home environment.
<br/><br/>
<b>Battery Status</b><br/>
See the estimated time until fully charged.
<br/><br/>
<b>Notifications</b><br/>
Display notifications from your favorite apps (requires Notification Access).
<br/><br/>
<b>Weather</b><br/>
Show current weather and a 5-day forecast from providers like OpenWeatherMap, Bright Sky, and Met.no.
<br/><br/>
<b>Extra Features</b><br/>
<ul>
  <li>Built-in flashlight.</li>
</ul>
<br/><br/>
<b>Open Source &amp; Donations</b><br/>
Nightdream is free, open-source software. The source code is available for review. Support development through donations.
<br/><br/>
<b>Permissions Explained:</b><br/>
<ul>
  <li><b>FOREGROUND_SERVICE:</b> Play radio in the background.</li>
  <li><b>MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS:</b> Silence device in night mode.</li>
  <li><b>WAKE_LOCK:</b> Keep screen on or wake device for alarms.</li>
  <li><b>READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:</b> Use custom background images.</li>
  <li><b>INTERNET &amp; ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE:</b> For weather, radio, and crash reports.</li>
  <li><b>RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED:</b> Reschedule alarms after reboot.</li>
  <li><b>VIBRATE:</b> Vibrate for alarms.</li>
  <li><b>SET_ALARM, USE_EXACT_ALARM, &amp; SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM:</b> Set alarms.</li>
  <li><b>ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION:</b> Get weather for your location.</li>
  <li><b>POST_NOTIFICATIONS:</b> Show alarm notifications.</li>
  <li><b>READ_MEDIA_AUDIO:</b> Use custom alarm sounds.</li>
  <li><b>SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW:</b> Display clock over other apps.</li>
  <li><b>FLASHLIGHT:</b> Use the flashlight.</li>
  <li><b>ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY:</b> Control Do Not Disturb.</li>
</ul>
