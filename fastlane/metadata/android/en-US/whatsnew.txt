- Welcome Nightdream to F-Droid! This is the first official release of our open-source desk clock and screensaver app.
- Dynamic digital clock with automatic brightness adjustment for day and night.
- Extensive customization options for colors, fonts, and analog watch faces.
- Full screensaver (Daydream) functionality for Android 4.2+.
- Quick alarms and integrated internet radio feature.
- Displays battery status, date, and new notifications.
- Optional current weather conditions and 5-day forecast from various open providers.
- Fully open-source and privacy-respecting.
- Includes optional Device Administrator permission for screen locking.
- Various performance improvements and bug fixes.
